Welcome to Lancet

Crafted with passion, served with love

At Lancet CAFFE, we believe coffee is more than a drink—it's a ritual, a moment of pause in a busy world. Our beans are sourced from sustainable farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Yemen, hand-roasted in small batches to preserve every nuance of flavor.

Step into our glass-walled sanctuary where natural light dances with the aroma of freshly ground beans. Every cup is a celebration of craftsmanship.
